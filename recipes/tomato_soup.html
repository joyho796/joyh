How to cook

Sear beef and onion 🥩

Beef should be cut in ~4cm cubes, onions diced. Put onions first until translucent then put in beef.


Add carrots, celery, flavorings 🥕

Carrots and celery should be ~1cm cubes. There should about the same amount of carrot and celery. Add a generous dollop of tomato paste.


Add tomatoes 🍅

Cut tomatoes into cubes (size doesn't really matter), add to pot.


Cover with water and cook 💦

Doesn't have to be boiling water, cover until everything is submerged. Pressure cook on high for 30 mins.


Add cabbage leaves and pastas 🥬

Taste and adjust soup with salt/sugar. Keep on a boil, add torn leaves and pasta and cook until pasta is ready.
