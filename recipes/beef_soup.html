How to cook

Sear beef

Beef should be cut in ~3cm cubes. Sear until browned. Doesn't need to be fully cooked through. Remove when done.


Saute onions

Onions roughly diced, saute until translucent.


Add carrots and tomatoes

Add diced carrots and tomatoes, bay leaves, and a pinch of sugar. Saute for ~2 mins.


Beef + sauce

Return beef to pot, add equal parts soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and cooking wine, ~75ml each.


Cover with water and cook

Doesn't have to be boiling water, cover until everything is submerged. Pressure cook on high for 30 mins.


Reduce and salt

Taste the soup, and reduce and salt as necessary.


Serve with noodles or rice
