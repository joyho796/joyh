Garlic Rice

Ingredients

Main things

5 cm cut fat pork belly
1 shallot
6 cloves garlic
1 cup cooked rice

Flavorings

butter
soy sauce
sugar

How to cook

Render out pork fat 🥓

Sear pork fat on low until cripsed and browned


Add butter and cook shallots and garlic 🧄

Add butter, then cook shallots until translucent, then add garlic, cook until slightly browned


Add soy sauce and sugar 🧉

Add ~60ml soy sauce, ~1 spoonful of brown sugar, cook until sugar is dissolved.

Add in rice, let crispen 🍚

Stir in rice until sauce is evenly distributed. Let sit for ~5 mins so rice can crispen on the bottom.
