Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

Main things

4 pcs bone-in chicken thigh
1 large carrot
4 stalks celery
1 onion
chicken stock/bone broth
elbow pastas

Flavorings

oregano
basil
salt

How to cook

Sear Chicken and onion 🧅

Put onions first until translucent then put in chicken. Should sear until browned.


Add carrots, celery, flavorings 🥕

Carrots and celery should be ~1cm cubes. There should about the same amount of carrot and celery.


Cover with chicken stock and cook 🍲

Cover until everything is submerged. Pressure cook on high for 20 mins.


Add pastas 🥬

Taste and adjust soup with salt/sugar. Keep on a boil and cook until pasta is ready.
