炒年糕

Ingredients

Main things

Flavorings

    

Key

How to cook

Marinate pork

Slice pork, marinate with small amount of soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and cornstarch. Mix well and set aside.


Prep ingredients

Soak the 年糕 in cold water. Dice shallots. Cut carrots into thin 丝儿.


Sear pork

Sear pork until browned and some fat has rendered out.


Add shallots and carrots

Add in shallots and carrots, saute ~2 mins. In the meantime break up individual 年糕s and drain the water.


Add 年糕

Add in the 年糕 and saute. Roughly chop the vegetables in the meantime. Add a little soy sauce and salt if necessary.


Add vegetables

After 年糕 browns a little, add in vegetables. Cook until desired softness.
