Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

Main things

2 skin on chickens
1 shallot
4 cloves garlic
200ml heavy cream
2 egg yolks
parmasean
pastas

Flavorings

oregano
thyme
black pepper

How to cook

Cook pasta until al dente

Thoroughly salt water. Whatever pasta choice is fine, save cup of pasta water.


Cook garlic and shallots

Cook garlic and shallots in oil until slightly browned.


Sear chicken

Search diced chicken until browned. Doesn't need to be cooked through.


Pour cream and add flavorings

Add in cream and all flavorings, heat on low until slightly bubbling.


Add pasta and pasta water

Add in and stir until thickens slightly.


Egg yolks and cheese

Turn off fire, let cool slightly. Add in egg yolks and cheese and mix thoroughly.
