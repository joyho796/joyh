Garlic Rice

Ingredients

Main things

5 cm cut fat pork belly
1 shallot
6 cloves garlic
1 cup cooked rice

Flavorings

butter
soy sauce
sugar

How to cook

Cook shallots and garlic 🧄

Add oil in pan, cook shallots until translucent, then add garlic


Sear chicken 🍗

Sear chicken until browned, doesn't have to be fully cooked.


Add soy sauce and sugar 🧉

Add ~100ml soy sauce, ~2 spoonfuls of brown sugar, cornstarch slurry. Cook until thickened. Top with sesame seeds.
